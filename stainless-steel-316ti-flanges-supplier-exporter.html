Stainless Steel 316TI is a stabilized grade of the 316 types, and it mostly recommended for higher temperature applications. This grade contains a minimal amount of 0.5% of Titanium. 316TI also includes Molybdenum, which offers resistance against corrosion, and pitting. Along with high-temperature suitability, it also provides resistance to acids such as hydrochloric acids, sulfuric acids, and acid sulphates. These additional metals make Stainless Steel 316TI stronger and more suitable for many critical applications.
